Why this fix was needed
-----------------------
The previous logic treated many general HR, Finance, Sales, and IT jobs as data science jobs because they mentioned words such as data, Excel, reporting, SQL, dashboard, or analytics.
The new logic only analyzes jobs with strong data science, data analytics, data engineering, machine learning, AI, or business intelligence role evidence.
Ambiguous jobs are marked as Review and are not included in the final skill-gap calculation.
