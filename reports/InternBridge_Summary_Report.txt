
InternBridge: Data Science Internship Skill Gap Analyzer

Project Summary
---------------
This project analyzes job postings to identify important skills required in the job market.
The dataset contains 1167 job posts and 5 job categories.

Dataset Columns
---------------
job_id, category, job_title, job_description, job_skill_set, skills_list, skill_count, description_word_count, combined_text, skills_text, student_job_match_score

Main Findings
-------------
1. Total number of job posts analyzed: 1167
2. Total number of job categories: 5
3. Number of data-related job posts identified: 1155
4. Percentage of data-related jobs: 98.97%
5. Average number of skills per job post: 17.85
6. Average job description word count: 516.82
